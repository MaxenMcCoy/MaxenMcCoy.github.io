The City by the Sea is a first person walk simulator set in an abandoned medieval fantasy city; a city forgotten by everyone except in myths.

The City by the Sea started out as the result of a week long game jam at Southern New Hampshire University in November of 2020, where it won second place. It was a really enjoyable project to work on, so after the jam I continued working on it in my free time. The project is a culmination of many of the variety of game development disciplines I've explored over the years, and an opportunity to explore a few new ones. This project is still in development, and is expected to be completed by the end of 2022.
