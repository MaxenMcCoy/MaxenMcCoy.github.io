Programmer, Designer - Made with a team for the NHTI Fall Game Jam 2018



PEAK is a sci-fi puzzle game set in an overgrown neon city. You play as a survivor trying to climb to the highest reaches of the city, in search of a mythic plant that may be able to cure the illness that has befallen your sibling.

This project was developed in 48 hours by a team of 3 students.
On the team, I was a programmer and puzzle designer; designing, programming, and implementing the mechanics of the game.
