Primary Developer - Made for the Very First Extra Credits Game Design Jam!



Do you like space? Do you like Pizza? Well, that's what Space Pizza is all about! Get in your rocket ship, and try to deliver as many pizzas to all the planets you can before the end of your shift!

I developed this project over 72 hours. Other than the art assets (sprites, music, and font assets), I developed this project on my own.
